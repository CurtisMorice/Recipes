Micah's Waffles

  • Time: 15 Minutes or so...Do you even cook bro?
  • Serves: How fat are you?
Ingredients---------------------------- Quantity
Flour 2 Cups (sifted)
Baking Powder 1 Table Spoon
Baking Soda 1 Table Spoon
Eggs 4 (seperated)
Butter 2 sticks (melted)
Yogurt (greek, plain or fage) 2 Cups

The Making of:
Begin by mixing the dry ingredients together.
Mix the yogurt and Yolks together then slowly add the melted butter.
Combine the egg, yogurt, butter mixture with the dry ingredients and stir only till combined. (Don't Beat)
Fold in egg whites! Don't stir, FOLD!!!
Now heat your waffle iron on high, pour the gooey batter in and cook em' like you want em'.

    • Gayle's Chocolate Balls

    • Time: 20 minutes or so...Do you even mix and chill bro?
    • Serves: How fat are you?
    Ingredients------------------------- Quantity
    Oreo's 2 bags (normal size)
    Cream Cheese 2 Packages (1 package for every bag of Oreo's)
    Chocolate (dark, milk, or flavored) 1 bag of chips or a 6-8 candy bars (ie Hersheys bar)
    Water or Milk 1/2 cup for keeping the chocolate from over cooking

    The Making of:

      Firstly crush the oreos or put them through a food processor.
      Secondly add the cream cheese and blend in to the oreos.
      Thirdly roll the mixture into balls. They can be watever size you like but I prefer a little smaller than a golf ball due to chocolate to oreo flavo ratio.
      Nextly take the chocoltae and put it in a double boiler. Once the chocolate is melted you can start dipping them balls in the "melty" and place on a tray with parchment or wax paper under neath. The milk or water is to keep the chocolate from drying out as it stays on the stove.
      Lastly place the trays in the fridgerator or freezer till the chocolate has dried and the balls are hard...at any point in these steps you can eat these fyi :)

    • My Morning Shake

    • Time: 20 minutes or so...Do you even blenderize bro?
    • Serves: 1 Peoples
    Ingredients------------------------- Quantity
    Milk or Water 2-3 Cups (depending on thickness and flavor)
    Spinach/Kale Mix 2 Handfulls
    Fruit of your choice 1 Cup
    Cinnamon 1 Tablespoon
    Chia, Flax Seed, BCAA's, etc. 1 Tablespoon
    Protien Powder Recommended Dosage

    The Making of:

      Mix all of the ingredients in a blender, turn on and blend till smoooooth...then poor in a glass. You're Welcome!
